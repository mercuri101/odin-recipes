Olivier

Description

The best salad in the world, very delicious and satiating

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Pickeld Cucumber
  • Green Peas
  • Bologna Sausage
  • Eggs
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt & Pepper

Note: instead of Bologna sausage, you can also use ham or chicken

Steps

  1. Boil-cook potatoes, carrots, and eggs.
  2. Cube all ingredients except peas and mix.
  3. Salt & pepper to taste.
  4. Add mayonnaise to taste.
