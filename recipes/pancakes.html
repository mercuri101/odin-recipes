Pancakes

Description

Classic pancakes. I love pancakes, like a lot...

Ingredients

  • Flour (1.5 cups)
  • Baking Powder (3.5 teaspoons)
  • Salt (1/4 teaspoon)
  • Sugar (1 tablespoon)
  • Milk (1.5 cup)
  • Egg
  • Melted Butter (3 tablespoons)

Note: One recipe yields 8 servings

Steps

  1. Sift together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. Pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth
  3. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the pan, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  5. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
