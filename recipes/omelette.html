Omelette

Description

The best option if you want something quick, satisfying, and customizable for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (2)
  • Water (30ml)
  • Salt & pepper (pinch)
  • Butter or oil
  • Filling ingredients of your choice

Note: Instead of water, you can also use milk or cream

Steps

  1. Mix and whisk eggs, water (or milk), salt and pepper.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled frying pan over a medium heat.
  3. Pour in the egg mixture. Keep the shape of omelette nice and plain
  4. When the mixture is still moist but rigid enough to keep its shape, cover half of omelette with fillings, and fold over unfilled side onto filled half.
  5. Cook for a minute, then slide omelette onto a plate.
